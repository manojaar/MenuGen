Logo Details: Banana Leaf

Breakfast
Upma Kozhukattai (Savory Steamed Rice Dumplings) and Thenga Kozhukattai (Sweet Coconut-Jaggery Dumplings) served with a simple Coconut Chutney.

Lunch
A curated Onam Sadya featuring: Matta Rice, Pineapple Rasam, Raw Banana Kalan, Mathanga Erissery (Pumpkin and Black-eyed Peas Curry), Cabbage Thoran, Beetroot Pachadi, Puli Inji, Pappadam, Sharkara Varatti, and Chakka Pradhaman (Jackfruit Payasam).

Dinner
Thenga Paal Kanji (Coconut Milk Rice Porridge) served with Chutta Chammanthi (Smoked Coconut Chutney) and Varuval (crispy fried sun-dried vegetables).
